The Game Of Murdery

---❤TIPS❤---

You can either play it online or go to some game rooms to play it face to face with your friends. I was once invited by some of my friends to visit a place famous for its murdery game. I didn't have any experience at that time, so I failed to find out who was the murderer in the end. The reason for my failure is that I was not aware that every player has a motivation to kill the victim. So if you are going to play this game, don't forget this!




Werewolf & UNO

---❤TIPS❤---

When talking about the 'Werewolf', there are some tips that I need to mention: the werewolves are always very good at concealing their identities! Sometimes they will kill the real fortuneteller and one of them will pretend to be this character. Be careful! Do not trust anyone unless you are pretty sure about it. If you are a witch, keep your potion and use it at a proper situation.
